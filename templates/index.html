Data-Driven Supply Chain Management

DD-SCM Project

From AI Revolutionaries

Data-Driven Supply Chain Management (DD-SCM) is proposed as a solution to increase efficiency in supply chain business processes. DD-SCM was created because there are still many processes that only rely on management without strong predictions, resulting in uncertain logistics estimates. DD-SCM aims to use data analysis to improve prediction accuracy, enabling optimal production and delivery planning. Business users can access information through this web dashboard to support better decision making and increase supply chain operational efficiency.
